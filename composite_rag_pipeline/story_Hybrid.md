## Introduction
Live Aid 1985 was organized to raise funds for famine relief in Ethiopia. It was inspired by the Band Aid charity single "Do They Know It's Christmas?" (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Do_They_Know_It%27s_Christmas) and conceived by Bob Geldof and Midge Ure. [1] Live Aid 1985 was a dual-venue global benefit concert. It was held on July 13, 1985, and broadcast to nearly 2 billion viewers worldwide (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Live_Aid#Background). [2]
